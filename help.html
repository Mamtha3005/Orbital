Help Page

What if I have not chosen any specialisation?

Chose the "no specialisation" option under "Enter your specialisation.

What if I have more than 1 specialisations?

Chose only 1 of your specialisations under the "Enter your specialisation". The other specialisation will be recorded under UE.

What if I am a Double-degree, Concurrent degree, Double Major student?

Our website is catered only for single degree students.
